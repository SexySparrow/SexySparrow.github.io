Description

"Battle of Stalingrad" is a First-Person Shooter game that presents the armed conflict between the two great powers of the twentieth century, Nazi Germany and the USSR. We aim to offer an experience that is as accurate as possible from a historical point of view. The player will fight for both the Wehrmacht and the Red Army. During the game the hero will take part in any kind of battle, such as armored attacks, dogfights, infantry fighting, covert operations and much more. Also, if desired, the player can access a virtual museum where he has the opportunity to read more about the battle itself and can observe the equipment of both camps in detail. The project is still in development, some features will be added as the project approaches the final stage.
